Nice to meet you!

Thank you for visiting my website! I'm Anne Pimra, an artistic and nature-focused creative designer dedicated to sustainable design solutions that combat climate issues and promote diversity, ensuring users live comfortably and cherish their environment.

Currently, I am a M.Arch student at the University of Toronto, Canada. Previously, I gained experience as a junior architectural designer, freelance creative designer, illustrator, and restaurant manager. These roles have provided me with opportunities to interact with diverse groups of people, including clients across various sectors, enriching my understanding of human perspectives.

My ambition as a designer is to mentor newcomers in the field and help cultivate a community that embraces sustainable design with artistic integrity. I am committed to promoting a design ethos that values nature and environmental stewardship.
